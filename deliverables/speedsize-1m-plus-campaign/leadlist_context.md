# Leadlist Context

## Company-Level Criteria
- E-commerce or media-heavy websites
- 1M+ monthly visits
- Active product catalogs with significant imagery or video

## Person-Level Criteria
- E-commerce directors, product managers, UX leads, CTOs, founders
- Decision-making authority over site performance and media infrastructure

## Sales Trigger
- High-traffic sites where media load speed directly impacts conversion and customer experience
- Sites with detectable CDN infrastructure (opportunity to position SpeedSize against current setup)

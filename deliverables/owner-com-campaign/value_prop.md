# Owner.com Value Proposition

## What Owner.com Does
Owner.com is an all-in-one restaurant marketing and online ordering platform that helps independent restaurants compete with national chains. It replaces third-party delivery apps (DoorDash, UberEats, Grubhub) with a direct ordering system, branded mobile app, and automated marketing — so restaurants keep their profits and own their customer data. Setup takes about a week.

## Offer Structure
- What is sold: Subscription platform (website builder, online ordering, branded app, marketing automation, zero-commission delivery)
- What prospect does: Gets a free demo, onboards with specialist in about a week
- Pricing: Monthly subscription, minimal delivery fees, no long-term contracts (month-to-month)

### Restaurant Owner / Operator

- Own your customer data and relationships instead of giving them to third-party apps
